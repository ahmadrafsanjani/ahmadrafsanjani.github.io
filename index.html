AiM is the Ai Matter research group that aims at transferring great ideas from Nature to robotics. We are part of the Center for Soft Robotics in SDU Biorobotics in the Maersk Mckinney Moller Institute at the University of Southern Denmark. We develop bioinspired soft materials with embodied intelligence and combine them with control and learning for soft robotics applications.

Ai Matter (AiM) research group aims at transferring great ideas from nature to robotics. We are part of the Center for Soft Robotics of SDU Biorobotics in the Maersk Mckinney Moller Institute at the University of Southern Denmark. We develop bioinspired soft materials with embodied physical intelligence and combine it with control and learning for soft robotics applications.

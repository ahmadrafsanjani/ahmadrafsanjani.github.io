AI Matter


Welcome to the AI Matter research group at the University of Southern Denmark. We aim at transferring great material ideas from Nature to robotics to build bioinspired soft robots with embodied intelligence and integrated control and learning. The AI Matter is an integral part of the Center for Soft Robotics in SDU Biorobotics at the Maersk Mckinney Moller Institute.

Principal Investigator


Ahmad Rafsanjani, Associate Professor, Center for Soft Robotics, SDU Biorobotics
